    • Dr. Kamran Khan

    Dr. Kamran Khan graduated from John Hopkins University with an M.D. and a specialty in prosthetic surgery. After working as a prosthetic surgeon at Massachusetts General Hospital he came on board with The Pallas Institute where he is our Chief Operating Officer, overseeing not only the surgical practice but also the Research and Development that goes on in order to bring cutting edge prosthesis to market.

    Dr. Mikaila Tran

    Dr. Mikaila Tran graduated from Stanford University with an M.D. and a speciality in Amputee Therapy. She worked as a therapist with the Mayo Clinic for many years, and joined The Pallas Institute as our Chief Executive Officer, where she oversees the financial and executive decisions of the practice, as well as the physical therapy program.

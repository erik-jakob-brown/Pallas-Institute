Physical Therapy

Here at Pallas Institute we believe an extensive physical therapy program administered by our knowledgeable, caring staff are the keys to a successful recovery. Each program is designed to target your specific mobility needs and provide comfort in helping you return to a more productive and active lifestyle.

Our staff of board certified doctors and physical therapists are dedicated to the full recovery of every patient. From the first visit, to the adjustments and maintenance of each prosthetic device for years to come. Initial consultations are free for all our wounded Military, Police, and Firefighters. We work with a wide range of insurance providers and there is with additional funding available for therapy sessions and prosthetic devices. Flexible appointments are available to fit a wide range of schedules.

At Pallas Institute we strive to touch one life at a time.
